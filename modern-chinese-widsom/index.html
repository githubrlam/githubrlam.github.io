About Our Show

Welcome to Chinese Modern Wisdom, a podcast where we explore contemporary issues in China through the lens of culture, society, and identity. Join us as we dive into thought-provoking topics and uncover the stories that shape modern Chinese life.

Latest Episode: Sham Marriages

In this episode of Chinese Modern Wisdom, we explore the complexities of sham marriages among queer men in China. Discover how societal pressures drive these men to marry straight women while concealing their sexual identities.

Join us for a deep dive into intersectionality, masculinity, and the challenges of navigating societal norms in China!

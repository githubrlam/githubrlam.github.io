About Our Show

Welcome to Chinese Modern Wisdom, a podcast where we explore contemporary issues in China through the lens of culture, society, and identity. Join us as we dive into thought-provoking topics and uncover the stories that shape modern Chinese life.

About This Episode

In this episode, we explore the immense pressure faced by men in China. From societal expectations to family obligations, men are often burdened with the responsibility of fulfilling traditional roles.

We discuss how these pressures manifest in their daily lives and the various ways men manage and cope with these challenges, shedding light on the cultural and social dynamics at play.

About Our Show

Welcome to Chinese Modern Wisdom, a podcast where we explore contemporary issues in China through the lens of culture, society, and identity. Join us as we dive into thought-provoking topics and uncover the stories that shape modern Chinese life.

Latest Episode: Sham Marriages

In this episode, we delve into the world of sham marriages, also known as 騙婚. These arrangements, where a gay man and a straight woman enter a heterosexual marriage, highlight the immense societal pressures faced by queer men in China. We discuss the cultural expectations, personal struggles, and the impact of these marriages on individuals and society.
